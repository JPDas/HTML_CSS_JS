Scrumbled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Using spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds(as if you are making an omlette).
  5. When the eggs still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
